Capstone Proposal

My topic is about the utopian future of public transportation. My focus is on the ideas of utopia, speculative design, and challenging mental models. My idea is to make a webpage replicating a map routing application, but there will be options for a future where there is very good public transportation in America. It will challenge the mental model that car dependency is a good or okay thing in the US.

Features of the website include being able to choose a starting point and destination. Then, there will be automatic routing. There will be options that show the current travel duration and methods in the present time, and then otions showing a future where there is maybe a good train system that replaces freeways. There oculd also be information showing how much pollution is caused and how much money is used.

I will have to look into libraries that will help make the map visualizations and the routings. I know Leaflet does maps, but I will need one that does the routes on the roads. My target audience is mainly the youth that live in cities like the bay area.
